Model Predictions

The structure is extracted from the CIF file and cleaned with c2x. This extra step was needed to create a Persistence Homology Images (PHImages), a descriptor related to the pore geometry. The PH image was used to predict (i) the Net Carbon Avoidance Cost relative to the MEA-based benchmark and (ii) the water resistance. Both models were trained for the cement case in the UK with TSA process.
